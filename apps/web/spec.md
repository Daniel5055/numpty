# Specification

DRAW - Draw up to 6 with the given cards

DRAWED - Acknowledge drawing

ATTACK - Attack with a card

ATTACKED - Player attacked with new card

DEFEND - Defend a card with a card

DEFENDED - Player's attack defended with new card

REVERSE - Reverse an attack

REVERSED - Player's attack reversed 

CONCEDE - Defender concedes

CONCEDED - Player's attack succeeds

FINISH - Finishes attack

FINISHED - Defender finished with defending

GRANT - Grant cards after conceding

GRANTED - Conceded defender granted cards
